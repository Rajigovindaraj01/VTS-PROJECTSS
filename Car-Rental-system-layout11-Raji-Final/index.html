Frequently Asked Questions
Everything You Need to Know Before You Hit the Road
What do I need to rent a Car ?
You need a valid driver's license, a government-issued ID, and a credit/debit card for payment. The minimum age is usually 21, depending on the car type. International renters may need extra documentation.
Can I rent a specific car model ?
Yes, you can choose a specific brand or model when booking, based on availability. We offer a wide range of options, including BMW, Audi, and MINI.Availability may vary, so booking early is recommended.
Is insurance included in the rental price ?
Basic insurance is included in the ritual. Additional coverage options are available for extra protection. Always check the details of your insurance coverage before confirming your booking.
Can I return the car at a different location ?
Yes, we offer one-way rentals between select locations. Additional fees may apply, and availability depends on the locations chosen. Check availability when booking your rental.
What happens if I need to cancel my reservation ?
you can cancel for up to 24 hours before pickup. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a fee. You can cancel directly through your account or by contacting customer support.

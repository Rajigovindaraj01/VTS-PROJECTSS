About Us

Indian weddings are a beautiful combination of colourful celebrations,age old traditions,festivals and intricate rituals.It is one of the most precious and priceless memories that we all embrace for all our lives and here at The Dream Wedding we aim to make those elaborate events as stunning and as memorable as possible for all of you!

We are a young team of wedding planners in Delhi, who entered the industry in 2016 with a desire to make your weddings exceptionally beautiful and worth remembering. We are a team of young individuals who have successfully organised numerous destination weddings and weddings in Delhi with the sole aim to make your dream a reality. From planning a hatke haldi to a happening cocktail and those lavish breakfast buffets, we give you all that and more!

The Dream Wedding takes care of every aspect of planning your Special day exactly as per your needs and budget. Our focus is always on the minutest details, the ones that convert a good wedding into a great one.We aim at delivering special experiences not just to you but to your loved ones too!

Planning a wedding is exciting but a tedious process and that's where we step in. Delhi weddings are all about the vibrant outfits, extravagant food spreads, lavish decorations,beautiful rituals and age old traditions, and we tailor-make each wedding to make sure you get them all because these memories last a lifetime. you can simply stay calm and enjoy when you have a wedding planner by your side

Destination weddings are quite a trend these days and everyone loves to take a break and make the most of their special day with their closed ones. We , at The Dream Weddings have been arranging destination weddings at locations like Goa,Kerala,jim corbett,Jaipur,Udaipur,Agra and even Thailand.From beach weddings to the royal ones, we love doing them all

Destination weddings usually have an intimate guest list while a local weddings calls for a grand reception with all the who's who of the town, and our team of young creative experts will make sure hear are blessings.

So all you need to do is recite your story to us and let the magic begin!

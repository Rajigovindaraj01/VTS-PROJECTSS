Our Recent Foods

Kancheepuram idly

Savor the divine taste of tradition with our Kanchipuram Idly, a Legendary delicacy from the ancient temples of Tamil Nadu. Unlike regular idlis, this special variety is spiced with black pepper, cumin, dry ginger and curry leaves giving it a unique aroma and flavour

Chettinad Kuzhambu

Chettinad Kuzhambu (Vegetarian Version) is a spicy,aromatic and flavourful South Indian Curry from the Chettinad region of Tamil Nadu. This cuisine is known for its bold spices, rich flavours, and rustic cooking style.

Elaneer Payasam

Elaneer Payasam (Tender Coconut Kheer) is a refreshing south Indian Dessert made using tender coconut water, Pulp and milk, It's light, mildy sweet and perfect for summer or festive occasions. Originating from Tamil Nadu and Kerala cuisine, this payasam is rich yet gentle on the plate.

About the Biryani Class
Chef Details

Unravel the magic of India's most iconic dish-biryani!

In this immersive, hands-on cooking course, you'll explore the art and science behind creating the perfect biryani-from selecting the right rice and spices to mastering the dum (slow cooking) technique. Whether you're a beginner or a home chef looking to level up, this course is designed to bring rich aromas, deep flavours, and authentic techniques into your kitchen

What Will You Learn?

Perfect 16 recipes covering different types of biriyani:

  1. Hyderabadi Biriyani
  2. Lucknowi (Awadhi) Biryani
  3. Kolkata Biryani
  4. Malabar Biryani
  5. Ambur Biryani
  6. Dindigul Biryani
  7. Thalassery Biryani
  8. Sindhi Biryani
  9. Bombay Biryani
  10. Kashmiri Biryani
  11. Chettinad Biryani
  12. Bohri Biryani
  13. Rawther Biryani
  14. Mughlai Biryani
  15. Veg Biryani (or Tehri)

Parent's Guidelines

At Magic Page, we're committed to creating a safe, enriching and imaginative reading experience for children. These guidelines are here to help parents and guardians make the most of our platform and ensure their child's online reading journey is both fun and secure.

1.Stay Involved

We encourage parents to read along with their children, especially younger readers. Shared reading helps build comprehension skills, encourages questions and turns storytime into quality bonding time.

2.Set Age-Appropriate Preferences

Use the parental controls to set your child's age and interests. This helps us recommend stories that are best suited to their reading level, maturity and imagination.

3.Monitor Screen Time

While we believe in the magic of stories, balance is important. Set reading time limits and take breaks to discuss the stories your child reads. This enhances learning and keeps screen time healthy.

4.Explore Together

Discover new genres, tales from around the world, and educational stories with your child. Reading together can spark curiosity and open up meaningful conversations.

5.Report Concerns

If you find any content that seems inappropriate or have suggestions for improvement, please let us know. We review all content carefully and value your feedback.

6.Protect Privacy

Please ensure your child does not share personal information on the site. All communication and data collection are designed to be COPPA-compliant and child-safe

"The Day the Sky Stopped Smiling"
Once, in a little town called Willowdrop, the sky was always smiling. Clouds danced, the sun beamed and kids played under the bright blue dome.
But one morning, everything changed.
The sky turned gray. Thunder growled like a hungry giant. And then, something strange happened- a giant crack split the clouds, and from it, a voice echoed:
"The world has stopped dreaming. If it does not dream again, the end will come.."
People panicked. Trees wilted. The birds stopped singing. It felt like Doomsday has arrived.
But while adults shouted and scientists started at screens, a group of children gathered in the library, whispering under a blanket of books and dust.

There was Leo the sketch artist, zara the storyteller, Mina the song maker and Ben, Who believed the stars could hear him.

"I think the world dreams through us",said zara."And maybe...We stopped dreaming too".
So, they began.

The voice returned, but this time, it laughed- a warm,rolling sound like thunder learning to smile.
"The dream is alive. Doomsday is not today."
And just likw that, Willowdrop was saved-not by warriors or machines but by a group of kids who remembered how to imagine.

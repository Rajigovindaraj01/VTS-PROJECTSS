Frequently Asked Questions

1. What happens if a transaction goes wrong?
If a transaction fails, your payment will be securely refunded, and our support team will help resolve the issue quickly.
2. Are there any restrictions on what I can sell?
Yes, you can only sell legal items such as bikes and related accessories. Any prohibited or unsafe items are not allowed.
3. What if the bike I buy isn't as described?
We offer buyer protection and dispute resolution to ensure fair outcomes and peace of mind.
4. How do I know if a bike is in good condition?
You can check detailed descriptions, photos, and request a mechanic inspection before finalizing the purchase.
5. Can I delete my listing after posting?
Yes, you can remove your listing anytime by going to your account dashboard and selecting "Delete Listing".
6. Can I sell bike parts or accessories?
Absolutely! You can list spare parts, helmets, and accessories as long as they follow our guidelines.
